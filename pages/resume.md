# **About Me**

Hi, I am **{{ site.author.name }}** :wave:,<br>

I am a predoctoral fellow in genetics, specializing in the computational analysis of genomic, epigenomic, and microbiome data. I hold a Master’s degree in Bioinformatics and Biostatistics, as well as a Master’s degree in Neuroscience, which together have provided me with a strong foundation in both advanced experimental techniques and quantitative data analysis. I have experience in RNA-seq and microarray data analysis, as well as in processing raw respiratory microbiome data and performing microbiome epigenome-wide association studies to investigate links between the respiratory microbiome and the blood or nasal epigenomes. I am a fast learner with a proven ability to adapt to new research areas and methodologies. My perseverance and attention to detail have enabled me to succeed in diverse scientific projects, and I am particularly interested in applying my expertise in omics data analysis to innovative research addressing complex biological challenges.
